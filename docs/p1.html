VOICE TETRIS 

CODE   LIVE


Voice Tetris is the game we know and love, but a bit more frustrating. (Just a little bit...)

Voice tetris is the result of trying to find ways to increase the level of fun (also frustration) you could get from playing tetris. As a kid, I used to love playing tetris and played a lot so this project is close to my heart. The idea of using sound as an input to play the game was really fascinating and I believe there is a lot of potential to it. Try the game on your phone! It is a lot of fun, but also very frustrating!

After cycling through many ideas on what to create, I decided to create something that is very simple but I believe would be very exciting. Tetris is a very popular game; there are even e-sports for them. So I thought to myself, will it not be funny to go to a tournament of tetris and see them screaming to their screen to change the orientations of the block.
